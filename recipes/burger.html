Burger

Description

A burger is the american version of a sandwich. It is normally made when the sun is out.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Bread
  • Greens

Method

  1. Fry your meat in a pan. Make sure to cook it through, or you may get ill.
  2. Toast your bread in the same pan that you cooked the meat in. This step is not entirely necessary, but it is how they do it in the movies.
  3. Construct your burger. First lay down some bread, then meat, then greens, then more bread.
  4. An additional optional step is to then add some cheese to your burger. This is then called a cheeseburger.
