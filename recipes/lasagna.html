Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is the italian version of a sandwich. It is normally made when there is no bread left.

Ingredients

  • Pasta sheets
  • Meat
  • Peas
  • Tomato sauce

Method

  1. Cook meat in a large pan until it goes brown. Salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Put half of the peas in the pan with the meat.
  3. Put the rest of the peas in the pan with the meat and the other peas.
  4. In a baking tray, put down a layer if pasta, then a layer of our meaty pea mixture, then a layer of tomato sauce.
  5. Repeat the previous step until its all gone.
  6. Cook him and eat!
