Soup

Description

Soup is the liquid version of a sandwich. It is normally made when there is no sandwiches left.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Veggies
  • Spoon

Method

  1. Heat up the water in a large pan.
  2. Keep heating up the water.
  3. Almost there.
  4. NOW! QUICK! Add your veggies to the water. It should be really hot by now.
  5. Leave this pot for a good three hours, or until your veggies become liquid.
  6. Eat with your spoon. This is important as forks do not work with this dish.
